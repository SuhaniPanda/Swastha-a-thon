Natural Remedies & Tips

  • Include iron-rich foods such as dark leafy greens, legumes, fortified cereals, eggs, and lean meats where culturally appropriate.
  • Pair iron sources with vitamin C (citrus, tomatoes, amla) to improve absorption.
  • Avoid tea/coffee immediately with iron-rich meals for better uptake.
  • For adolescents, consult a health professional before starting supplements.
